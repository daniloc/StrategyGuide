Welcome to the 21st Century Strategy Guide

There's no precedent to help you understand the world that's coming.

But the future is understandable. The 21st Century Strategy Guide is a primer on everything you need to know about how the future works, and how to make your way into it.

You won’t find anything about how to code in here. That’s an adventure unto itself, and unless you’re really excited to walk that path, I’m not going to insist you need it to succeed. Still, we will explore how code is animating our world, and changing its rules.

But there’s more to the 21st century than computer code. Constant connectedness is changing the rules of every game humans play. We’ll walk through the mutations of everything from friendship to business and politics, as fast social technology gobbles up and changes them all.

This is all the information I wish I'd had at the age of 17, as I figured out how to make my way in the world. I hope it's as helpful to you as it would have been for me.

Let’s get moving.
